Recipe#1

    How to make the perfect boiled eggs:

  • time: 25 Minutes
  • Serves: 6-12
  • Ingredients Quantity
    egg the number of eggs you want and it depends on the size of your pot
    water pour water until it fully covers the eggs
    a pot 1
    gas stove 1

    Step1: Add water

    Step3: Place eggs

    Step4: Cover the eggs with an inch of water

    Step5: Heat on high

    Step6: Bring to roiling boil

    Step7: Turn off the heat

    Step9: Cover the pot

    Step10: Wait for 10-12 minutes

    Step11: Strain the hot water from the pot and run cold water over the eggs to cool them quickly

    Step12: Enjoy :)
